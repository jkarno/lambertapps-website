Last updated: April 23, 2026

2. What Trot is

Trot is a step-tracking app for iPhone with a virtual pet companion, built by Lambert Apps. It reads Health and motion data from your device to count steps, display stats, and react to your activity. Trot is for personal, informational, and entertainment use. It is not a medical device.

3. Health data disclaimer

Step counts, distance, flights climbed, active minutes, and other metrics shown in Trot are estimates derived from Apple Health and your device's motion sensors. Accuracy varies based on how you carry your phone, sensor calibration, and other factors. Trot should not be used for medical decisions. If something feels off with your health, talk to a healthcare professional.

4. Trot Pro subscription

Trot offers an optional subscription called Trot Pro that unlocks:

  • An ad-free experience
  • Home screen widgets
  • Full walking history and year/month Trends views
  • All future Pro features as they ship

Subscription terms:

  • Trot Pro is offered on an annual or monthly basis. Current pricing is shown in the app and on the App Store.
  • The annual plan includes a 7-day free trial for first-time subscribers. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, you will be charged for the first year.
  • Any unused portion of a free trial is forfeited when the trial converts to a paid subscription.
  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically at the same price unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Your Apple ID will be charged within 24 hours before renewal.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
